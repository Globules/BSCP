## Labs tips

Every lab from race conditions has:

- endpoints with rate limits or single-use actions
- actions involving tokens (reset password, confirmation, etc.)
- state changes occurring in multiple steps
- possibility to send multiple requests in parallel (Repeater / Turbo Intruder)
